# Collective Hive Executive Summary

**Date:** March 3, 2026  
**Headquarters:** Ottawa, Ontario, Canada  
**Stage:** Early validation / pre-scale

## Company Overview

Collective Hive is a membership-based operations platform for small businesses and solo operators. We combine workflow software, AI-assisted operations, and partner-delivered professional services to reduce administrative burden and operating costs.

**Core value proposition:**
- One membership
- One support desk
- One coordinated operating layer for business housekeeping

## Problem

Small businesses face recurring operational friction:
- Heavy admin load (invoicing, records, compliance prep)
- Fragmented service providers (accounting, legal, web)
- Limited purchasing leverage
- Poor visibility across tools and vendors

This reduces owner focus, slows growth, and increases cost.

## Solution

Collective Hive centralizes key operational workflows and coordinates delivery through vetted licensed partners where required.

**Initial service scope:**
- Invoicing and bookkeeping coordination
- Legal template access and discounted partner consults
- Managed web/hosting operations
- Procurement support and member pricing opportunities

**Operating principle:**
AI handles repetitive tasks; humans handle approvals and regulated decisions.

## Business Model

- Recurring subscription memberships (monthly and annual)
- Tiered plans (Starter, Growth, Scale)
- Add-on service blocks for advanced needs
- Asset-light specialist delivery through partner network

## Target Market

**Initial market:** Ottawa-region solo operators and small businesses (1–25 employees).  
**Expansion path:** broader Ontario footprint, then selected US markets after operational repeatability is proven.

## Why We Can Win

- Integrated service orchestration rather than disconnected tools
- Outcome-based delivery focus (time saved + cost saved)
- Lean, automation-first operating model with partner leverage
- Clear accountability through one member relationship

## Financial Direction (Illustrative)

Economics improve significantly with scale if retention and service consistency are maintained.

**Primary performance levers:**
- Net member growth
- Churn control
- Gross margin discipline
- Automation and standardized workflows
- CAC payback efficiency

## Risk Management

**Primary risks:**
- Service quality variability
- Regulatory boundaries in legal/accounting/financial workflows
- Partner dependency

**Mitigation approach:**
- SLA contracts and partner scorecards
- Licensed-partner model for regulated services
- Multi-partner redundancy by category
- Auditability and human-in-loop governance for AI-assisted work

## Near-Term Milestones (0–12 Months)

1. Finalize partner agreements and service SOPs
2. Launch MVP membership offer in Ottawa
3. Onboard pilot cohort and validate measurable outcomes
4. Standardize onboarding and support SLAs
5. Scale to repeatable member growth with strong retention

## Current Ask / Collaboration Opportunities

- Strategic service partners (accounting, legal, web operations)
- Pilot members in Ottawa
- Advisors with SMB operations, compliance, and go-to-market expertise
- Optional early-stage capital aligned with disciplined, retention-first scaling

## Contact Positioning Statement

Collective Hive helps small businesses focus on their core work by taking operational housekeeping off their plate through one coordinated membership experience.
