# Collective Hive Service Offer Definition (v1)

Date: March 3, 2026  
Status: Working baseline for sales, onboarding, and partner alignment

---

## 1) Core Offer in One Sentence

Collective Hive is a membership platform that handles small-business housekeeping through one coordinated operations layer so owners can focus on their core work.

---

## 2) Primary Customer

### Ideal member profile (v1)
- Solo operators and small companies (roughly 1–25 employees)
- High admin burden, low internal ops capacity
- Need affordable professional support and better operational structure

### Initial geography
- Ottawa first, then broader Ontario once repeatability is proven

---

## 3) What We Offer (v1 Scope)

## A) Back-Office Operations
- Invoicing and billing workflow coordination
- Bookkeeping process coordination and readiness support
- Document organization and recurring admin workflow management

## B) Legal Access (Partner-Delivered)
- Legal template access and structured intake support
- Discounted legal consult routing through licensed partners
- Request tracking, escalation, and status visibility

## C) Web and Digital Operations
- Managed web hosting support
- Routine website updates and operational maintenance
- Basic ecommerce/POS integration coordination (by plan)

## D) Procurement and Member Savings Support
- Vendor quote comparison support
- Member pricing and sourcing assistance for common business needs
- Savings tracking support where data is available

---

## 4) What We Do Not Offer in v1

- We are not acting as an insurance carrier
- We are not replacing licensed legal or accounting professionals
- We are not running pooled client-fund commingling structures
- We are not launching every possible service at once

---

## 5) Delivery Model

- One member relationship and one support desk
- AI-assisted operations for speed and consistency
- Human approvals for regulated or high-risk decisions
- Licensed partners deliver regulated work
- SLA-driven routing, escalation, and accountability

---

## 6) Membership Packaging (Current Draft)

- Starter (solo-focused essentials)
- Growth (small-team operational support)
- Scale (higher support limits + priority handling)

Billing structure:
- Monthly and annual options
- Annual plan positioned as better value

---

## 7) Core Value Promises

- Save owner/operator admin time
- Reduce operating friction and service fragmentation
- Improve access to professional support at member pricing
- Provide clearer operational visibility and accountability

---

## 8) Success Metrics (Offer-Level)

- Onboarding completion time
- First response and resolution SLA performance
- Member churn and retention trend
- Member-reported time saved
- Member-reported cost savings / value realization

---

## 9) Sales Positioning Statement

You run your business. Collective Hive runs your business housekeeping through one coordinated membership model.

---
