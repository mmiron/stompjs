# Collective Hive Partner Outreach Script (Accounting / Legal)

## Intro Email
Subject: Ottawa SMB partnership opportunity (qualified recurring client flow)

Hi [Name],

I’m building Collective Hive, a membership platform that helps small businesses by coordinating back-office operations. We are selecting a small number of licensed accounting and legal partners for an initial Ottawa launch.

We expect to deliver qualified recurring client flow with standardized intake and clear SLAs.

Would you be open to a 20-minute discussion this week to review fit, pricing model, and capacity?

Best,
[Your Name]

## Discovery Call Agenda (20 minutes)
1. Firm focus and ideal client profile
2. Turnaround times and SLA readiness
3. Fixed-fee/packaged pricing options
4. Team capacity and growth tolerance
5. Quality controls and escalation process
6. Pilot structure (90-day)

## Qualification Questions
- What SMB segments do you serve best?
- Can you support standardized intake templates and workflow tooling?
- What service bundles can be priced predictably?
- What are your response/resolution commitments?
- How do you handle peak load periods?
- What quality controls do you use?

## Pilot Proposal Elements
- 90-day pilot term
- Expected monthly lead volume range
- Preferred pricing schedule
- SLA terms and scorecard metrics
- Exit and transition terms
