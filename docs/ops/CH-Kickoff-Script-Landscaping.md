# Kickoff Call Script — Landscaping Members

Duration: 30 minutes  
Goal: Establish scope, capture workflow baseline, and lock first 30-day wins

---

## 0–3 min: Opening and Context
- Thanks for joining and quick introductions
- Confirm objective: reduce admin burden and improve billing consistency
- Confirm call outcome: clear onboarding plan + immediate next actions

Prompt:
- “By day 30, what would make you say this membership is already worth it?”

---

## 3–8 min: Business Snapshot
Capture:
- Service mix (recurring maintenance vs one-time jobs)
- Team size and owner involvement in admin
- Seasonality pattern and peak months

Prompts:
- “Which part of billing/admin steals the most time each week?”
- “Where do delays usually happen today?”

---

## 8–15 min: Current Workflow Mapping
Map current process:
1. Job completed
2. Invoice issued
3. Payment follow-up
4. Reconciliation

Capture:
- Tools currently used
- Where information gets lost
- Typical invoice lag and collection lag

Prompts:
- “How soon after service do invoices currently go out?”
- “What % of invoices need follow-up more than once?”

---

## 15–20 min: Scope and Service Boundaries
Confirm what Collective Hive will handle in v1:
- Invoicing cadence coordination
- Recurring billing schedule setup
- Admin reminders/checklists
- Partner intake coordination where needed

Confirm out-of-scope for now:
- Highly custom finance/legal work outside agreed lane

Prompt:
- “Any must-have tasks you want in month one?”

---

## 20–25 min: First 30-Day Plan (Live)
Set concrete commitments:
- Week 1: import customer list + billing schedule
- Week 2: configure recurring invoicing and reminders
- Week 3: run first consistency review and AR cleanup
- Week 4: deliver first ops health snapshot and optimization list

Assign owners and deadlines for each action.

---

## 25–30 min: Close and Next Steps
Confirm:
- Access and data checklist deadline
- First working session date
- Weekly check-in cadence
- Escalation contact

Close line:
- “Our first quick win is getting your invoice rhythm stable and visible. We’ll report progress in week one.”

---

## Required Data Checklist (Send After Call)
- Customer list/export
- Current recurring schedule
- Open invoices/aging report
- Accounting export/access
- Website/hosting access (if applicable)

---

## Success Criteria for This Segment (30-Day)
- Faster invoice issuance cadence
- Reduced overdue follow-ups
- Clear recurring billing calendar
- Owner-reported admin time reduction
