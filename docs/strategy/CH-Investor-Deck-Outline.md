## Slide 1 — Cover

- **Company:** Collective Hive
- **Tagline:** Business housekeeping for companies that want to grow
- **Presenter:** Founder
- **Location:** Ottawa, Ontario, Canada

**Speaker note:**
We are building a membership platform that removes operational burden from small businesses by coordinating essential services in one place.

---

## Slide 2 — Problem

### Small businesses face 4 core pain points
- Admin overload steals owner time
- Professional services are fragmented and expensive
- Local businesses have weak purchasing power
- Tool sprawl creates poor visibility and compliance risk

**Speaker note:**
Most small businesses don’t fail from lack of demand—they stall because operations become chaotic and expensive.

---

## Slide 3 — Solution

### Collective Hive membership platform
- One membership
- One support desk
- One operating system for back-office workflows

### Initial service coverage
- Invoicing/bookkeeping coordination
- Legal template + partner consult access
- Managed web/hosting operations
- Procurement support for member savings

**Speaker note:**
We are not just software and not just services. We orchestrate both.

---

## Slide 4 — Product Experience

### How it works
1. Join a plan
2. Onboard into standardized workflows
3. Operate with AI-assisted and partner-delivered support
4. Receive monthly business health reports

### Member outcome promise
- Less admin time
- Better service access
- Lower operating costs

## Slide 5 — Why Now

- SMBs are increasingly open to outsourced operations
- Rising compliance complexity increases need for coordinated support
- Cost pressure makes pooled buying power more attractive

## Appendix B — Risk and Mitigation Summary

### Risks
- Service quality variability
- Regulatory boundaries in financial/legal flows
- Partner dependency
- Customer trust and retention

### Mitigations
- SLA governance and scorecards
- Clear legal boundaries and licensed partner model
- Multi-partner redundancy per category
- Outcome transparency and monthly business health reporting
