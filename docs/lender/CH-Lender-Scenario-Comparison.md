## 3) Lender-Relevant Interpretation

### Low Case
- Stress scenario shows runway pressure and negative cash by month 24.
- Indicates need for tighter hiring gates, stronger retention, ARPM lift, or additional capital.

### Base Case
- Near break-even EBITDA by end of Year 2.
- Retains positive ending cash in current assumptions.
- Represents disciplined, controlled scaling path.

### High Case
- Strong Year 2 operating leverage and cash rebuild.
- Demonstrates upside if growth, retention, and margin execution all hold.
