Populate this document alongside your accounting model or spreadsheet and keep all assumptions consistent with:

- Member growth and churn assumptions
- Partner delivery costs

## 3) 24-Month Monthly Projection Template

### Column definitions
- Active Members Start: prior month ending active members
- New Members: new paid members added in month
- Churned Members: active members lost in month
- Active Members End: start + new - churned
- Revenue: active members end x ARPM
- COGS/Partner Delivery: revenue x (1 - gross margin)
- Gross Profit: revenue - COGS
- Opex: payroll + software + admin + marketing + other
- EBITDA (proxy): gross profit - opex
- Debt Service: principal + interest (if any)
- Net Cash Change: EBITDA - debt service - capex/one-time costs
- Ending Cash: prior ending cash + net cash change

## 7) Lender FAQ Prompts (Prepare Before Meetings)

1. How sensitive is cash flow to churn increases?
2. At what member count do you reach monthly break-even?
3. Which costs scale directly with member growth vs fixed?
4. What is your fallback plan if growth is 30% below plan?
5. What hiring is deferrable without damaging service quality?
6. What are your largest concentration risks?
7. How do you ensure regulated service boundaries are respected?
8. What controls protect client data and financial workflows?
