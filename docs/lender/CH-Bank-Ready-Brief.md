# Collective Hive Bank-Ready Brief

**Date:** March 3, 2026  
**Company:** Collective Hive  
**Location:** Ottawa, Ontario, Canada  
**Purpose:** Credit/lending discussion support document

## 1) Business Summary

Collective Hive is a recurring-revenue membership business serving small companies and solo operators. The company provides coordinated business operations support (invoicing/bookkeeping workflows, legal-access coordination, managed web operations, and procurement assistance) through a combination of internal operations software, AI-assisted workflows, and licensed third-party partners.

## 2) Revenue Model and Predictability

### Core revenue sources
- Monthly/annual membership subscriptions
- Add-on service blocks
- Optional project-based operational support

### Predictability features
- Recurring subscription structure
- Annual plan mix to improve visibility
- Defined service scope and SLA framework
- Standardized onboarding and renewal process

## 3) Cost Structure and Margin Controls

### Major cost categories
- Payroll for core operations team
- Partner-delivered service costs (accounting/legal support)
- Technology stack (CRM, helpdesk, automation, AI APIs)
- Customer acquisition spend

### Margin protection mechanisms
- Standardized service packages (limit custom delivery drift)
- Automation-first workflows to reduce labor intensity
- Partner SLAs with pricing schedules
- Multi-partner model to reduce vendor concentration risk

## 4) Cash Flow Discipline

### Working capital practices
- Subscription billing for recurring cash inflows
- Monthly operating review against budget and margin targets
- Controlled hiring tied to member and workload thresholds
- Expense controls on software and discretionary spend

### Policy targets (internal)
- CAC payback target: under 4 months
- Gross margin target: above 50% at maturity stage
- Churn threshold monitoring: monthly logo churn target under 3–4%

## 5) Risk Profile and Mitigations

### Key risks
- Service quality variability
- Regulatory exposure if operating outside licensed boundaries
- Customer concentration or elevated churn
- Partner dependency in regulated categories

### Mitigation controls
- Regulated services delivered only by licensed partners
- Written partner contracts with SLA and escalation clauses
- Standard operating procedures and quality scorecards
- Centralized ticketing, audit logs, and KPI reporting
- Redundancy: minimum of two partners per critical category

## 6) Regulatory and Funds-Flow Approach

### Current operating stance
- Members retain their own legal entities and bank accounts
- Collective Hive coordinates workflows; does not rely on pooled client fund commingling
- Payment and account movements are orchestrated through established rails and permissions

### Benefit to lender
This reduces legal/regulatory risk versus high-complexity umbrella/pooling models and supports cleaner auditability.

## 7) Growth Plan and Repayment Capacity Narrative

### Growth sequence
1. Ottawa MVP launch with focused service scope
2. Operational repeatability and retention validation
3. Ontario expansion after KPI thresholds are met
4. US market entry only after mature unit economics

### Repayment capacity logic
- Recurring revenue base scales with member growth
- Margin expansion expected as automation and SOP maturity increase
- Controlled expansion model prioritizes retention over rapid burn

## 8) Governance and Reporting Package (for lender review)

Collective Hive can provide monthly reporting cadence including:
- Active members and net growth
- MRR/ARR trendline
- Churn and retention metrics
- Gross margin and operating margin trend
- CAC and CAC payback
- Cash runway and budget variance
- Aging of receivables and subscription collections

## 9) Capital Use Framework (if credit facility requested)

Proposed use of debt capital:
- Working capital buffer for onboarding and service scaling
- Automation/workflow infrastructure improvements
- Controlled go-to-market expansion in validated segments

Not intended for:
- High-risk regulatory model expansion
- Unbounded hiring without KPI triggers

## 10) Credit Discussion Summary

Collective Hive is designed as a disciplined recurring-revenue operations platform with risk-managed partner delivery, standardized service controls, and measurable unit economics. The company’s approach prioritizes predictability, margin governance, and controlled expansion, supporting a practical framework for responsible lending discussions.
