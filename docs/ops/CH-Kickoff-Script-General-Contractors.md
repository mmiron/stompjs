# Kickoff Call Script — General Contractors

Duration: 35 minutes  
Goal: Map quote-to-cash process, identify billing friction, and launch first AR/process improvements

---

## 0–4 min: Opening and Objective
- Quick introductions and role clarity
- Confirm objective: improve billing flow, collections rhythm, and admin reliability
- Confirm call outcome: 30-day plan + owner-level action list

Prompt:
- “If this works in 30 days, what problem should be noticeably better?”

---

## 4–10 min: Business and Project Snapshot
Capture:
- Number of active projects
- Typical job cycle and invoice milestones
- Team roles handling estimates, billing, and follow-up

Prompts:
- “Where does quote-to-cash break down most often?”
- “What paperwork causes the most delays?”

---

## 10–18 min: Workflow Baseline (Quote -> Deposit -> Progress -> Final)
Map current workflow and timing:
- Estimate creation
- Deposit invoicing
- Progress billing events
- Final invoice and closeout

Capture failure points:
- Missing change-order documentation
- Late progress invoices
- Inconsistent collections follow-up

Prompts:
- “Average days from work completed to invoice sent?”
- “What % of projects have complete billing docs at close?”

---

## 18–24 min: Scope Alignment and Partner Lanes
Confirm v1 in-scope:
- Billing cadence coordination and AR follow-up process
- Document workflow and checklist discipline
- Legal/accounting intake coordination where needed

Confirm boundaries:
- Regulated services delivered through licensed partners
- Custom legal/accounting engagements as scoped add-ons

---

## 24–31 min: First 30-Day Execution Plan
Set concrete actions:
- Week 1: import AR aging + active pipeline + templates
- Week 2: standardize estimate/invoice milestones
- Week 3: run first AR cleanup cycle and overdue action plan
- Week 4: deliver operations summary and next-step improvements

Assign owners and deadlines live on call.

---

## 31–35 min: Close and Next Steps
Confirm:
- Data/access checklist completion date
- First project-admin review session
- Weekly check-in schedule
- Escalation point of contact

Close line:
- “Our first measurable win is improving AR discipline and invoice cycle time with clear project-level checkpoints.”

---

## Required Data Checklist (Send After Call)
- Open AR aging report
- Active projects and billing stage list
- Estimate and invoice template set
- Change-order tracking format/docs
- Accounting export/access
- Vendor/subcontractor reference list (optional but useful)

---

## Success Criteria for This Segment (30-Day)
- AR aging reduction trend begins
- Progress invoice consistency improves
- Project billing documentation completeness increases
- Owner-reported reduction in billing/admin friction
